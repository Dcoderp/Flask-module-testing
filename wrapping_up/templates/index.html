Learn more about Wally West

Wally West, also known as The Flash, is one of the most iconic speedsters in the DC Universe. As the nephew of Barry Allen's wife, Iris West, Wally gained his powers in a freak accident similar to Barry's. He became Kid Flash and later took on the mantle of The Flash after Barry's sacrifice during the Crisis on Infinite Earths. Wally is known for his incredible speed, his connection to the Speed Force, and his unwavering dedication to protecting the world.
